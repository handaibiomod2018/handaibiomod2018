Design

Principal of the design

   The structure takes a shape of a 2D triangle. The sides of the triangle will then bo connected to other triangles by several methods. Firstly, The connection will be based on staples that base pair with the scaffold strand of both triangles. Second, weaker pairing by using protruding strands complementary to each others on the sides of the triangles. Finally, aptamers that can conformationally change will be used to initiate connection


   Furthermore, 2 type of similar nanorobots will be attempted. The first one being the activator, which can recognize ligand on the solution and then initiate association with the effectors. The effectors will be the second type of nanorobots that will be present in higher concentration and will create a positive feedback mechanism to amplify the signal initiated by the activator.
